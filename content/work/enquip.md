# Enquip – Real Estate / FS

Enquip software enables real estate owners and investors to analyse portfolio sustainability, compare actionable clean energy options, and take action to increase property values.

## The Challenge

Real estate owners and investors needed a comprehensive platform to:
- Analyse portfolio sustainability metrics
- Compare actionable clean energy options
- Make data-driven decisions to increase property values
- Navigate the complex landscape of sustainable real estate investment

## Our Solution

Using our **5-Day Prototype** system, we took Enquip's existing platform and developed a more intuitive, efficient version that enhanced its capabilities and was more attractive to customers.

Our approach included:
- **UI/UX Design**: Creating a beautifully simple interface
- **Rapid Prototyping**: 5-day sprint methodology
- **Energy-focused Illustrations**: Visual communication of sustainability concepts
- **Landing Page Optimization**: Converting visitors to users

## The Results

Enquip's new "beautifully simple" platform immediately helped the company:
- **Raise investment** from new funding sources
- **Sell to new customers** through improved user experience
- **Enhance platform capabilities** while maintaining simplicity
- **Increase user adoption** through intuitive design
